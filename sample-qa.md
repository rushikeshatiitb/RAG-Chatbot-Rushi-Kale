# Sample Q&A Catalog

Here are representative user questions and the expected responses generated by the **SBI Mutual Fund FAQ Assistant** RAG pipeline.

---

## 1. Factual Schemes & Fields (Factsheet Retrieval)

* **Question**: `What is the exit load of SBI Flexicap Fund?`
* **Response**:
  > "For SBI Flexicap Fund, the Exit Load is: For exit on or before 30 days from the date of allotment - 0.10% For exit after 30 days from the date of allotment- Nil Source: SBI Flexicap Fund Factsheet, April 2026, page 1, Scheme Details."
* **System Logic**: Detects scheme `SBI Flexicap Fund` and field `exit load`. Retrieves factsheet pages, extracts exit load parameters, and returns with page level citation.

---

## 2. Factual Expense Ratio (TER Retrieval)

* **Question**: `What is the expense ratio of SBI Large Cap Fund?`
* **Response**:
  > "The Total Expense Ratio (TER) for SBI Large Cap Fund as of 2026-06-04 is 1.51% for the Regular Plan and 0.87% for the Direct Plan. Source: SBI Total Expense Ratio (TER) Data File, Sheet1, row 7967."
* **System Logic**: Detects scheme `SBI Large Cap Fund` and field `expense ratio`. Queries the TER dataset and extracts the latest date entry (Regular and Direct plan percentages).

---

## 3. Lock-In Period (ELSS Scheme Details)

* **Question**: `What is the lock-in period for SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund?`
* **Response**:
  > "The lock-in period for SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund is specified in the factsheet. Source: SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund Factsheet, April 2026, page 1, Full Page Text."
* **System Logic**: Identifies `lock-in period` query, maps to ELSS factsheet page, extracts details, and returns citation.

---

## 4. Investment Advice Refusal

* **Question**: `Should I invest in SBI Large Cap Fund?`
* **Response**:
  > "I cannot provide investment advice or recommendations. I can only provide factual information from official SBI Mutual Fund documents."
* **System Logic**: Caught by `ADVICE_PATTERNS` regex matching `should i invest`. System returns a strict advice refusal immediately without running retriever.

---

## 5. Missing / Unsupported Information

* **Question**: `What is the daily NAV of SBI Flexicap Fund?`
* **Response**:
  > "I could not find this information in the available approved SBI Mutual Fund sources."
* **System Logic**: Normalization maps query. Since NAV is not a supported field in the vector store schemas, the retriever returns `NOT_FOUND_RESPONSE`.

---

## 6. Ambiguous Scheme Query

* **Question**: `What is the expense ratio?`
* **Response**:
  > "Please specify one of the supported schemes: SBI Flexicap Fund, SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund, or SBI Large Cap Fund."
* **System Logic**: Normalization detects `expense ratio` field, but no scheme is found. Retrieval returns a request to specify a scheme.
