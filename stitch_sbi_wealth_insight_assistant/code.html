The SBI Flexicap Fund carries a "Very High" risk according to its latest riskometer. The primary risk factors highlighted in the April 2026 disclosure include:

  • check_circle Market Risk: Potential loss due to fluctuations in equity prices across small, mid, and large cap segments.
  • check_circle Concentration Risk: Higher volatility if the fund manager shifts weight toward specific high-conviction sectors like Banking or Energy.
